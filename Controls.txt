Timeless Record Player Controls:
--------------------------------
Revealing album - Scroll with mouse wheel

Revealing vinyl - Click on the revealed cover

Moving vinyl - Click, hold and drag

Playing music - Dragging the vinyl onto the record player and releasing the mouse button

Putting the vinyl back	- Click and hold the vinyl on the record player, move it to the revealed album
			- Click the revealed album (faster option)

Toggle displaying instructions (if distracting) - Press <I>
--------------------------------
